We are GameCloud

Designing, Coding and Playing the Games We Love!

The new GameCloud Studio is headquartered in beautiful Murrieta, just a stone's throw from San Diego, Orange County and Los Angeles! Temecula is best known for its colorful hot air balloons, award winning wineries and championship golf courses settled in beautiful rolling hills. The city is just over 30 square miles on the north side of San Diego County offering a historic Downtown district, year-round entertainment and recreation, and an average temperature in the mid 70s. Our fine weather and artistic setting is perfect for...GAMES!

The Model

GameCloud Studios provides full service game development for approximately 50% of the cost of North American teams while maintaining equivalent quality and predictability. We simplify customer interaction with our North American leadership team which provides hands-on production, project management, design, art leadership, and engineering oversight. This is combined with the China cost structure and scale. The GameCloud teams have extensive experience with multi-cultural distributed development, and have established proven methodology to maximize efficiency. Our unique model offers us the ability to provide the best practices of East and West all the while maintaining rigorous checks and balances to ensure the highest quality delivery. The GameCloud developers all have experience with a variety of development methodologies. The leadership team will work with you to define the right process for your project.

Online Game Dev

GameCloud is always exploring new ideas. We offer cradle-to-grave game development for a variety of online formats with an emphasis on social network and downloadable micro-transaction games across platforms. We have extensive experience with both licensed titles as well as original IP -- as long as it's fun!

Tying to bring your game to Asia? Ask us about our co-development services!

Ports

At GameCloud we can convert your console and PC games to online. We have extensive experience with new formats and business models and can provide value added custom content, online features and internationalization. Above all we respect and protect your intellectual property.

Outsourcing

Augmentum's team of over 1,000 developers in China can be put to work expanding your game and building core technology. Build and test automation as well as qualitative and quantitative play test are also available. Our augmentation services are all highly scalable to your needs.
