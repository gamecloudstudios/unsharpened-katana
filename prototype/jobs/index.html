Come Work for Us!

GameCloud is seeking creative leaders ready to shape the future of online games in our Murrieta, California studio. Murrieta offers an excellent quality of life, great schools, and a fantastic location, nestled centrally between San Diego, Orange County and Los Angeles. GameCloud offers competitive compensation and benefits, and an incredible opportunity to grow with a passionate, dynamic organization. Interested in joining our team? We are now accepting applications for experienced Producers, Technical Directors, Game Developers, Flash Developers, Game Designers, 2-D and 3-D Artists and Project Managers.

Through a combination of internal development teams and second party partnerships, we are committed to developing innovative and engaging hit games for mobile. By applying our creative energy to solid technologies we are developing fun and engaging games for large and ever-expanding groups of loyal fans. We're in the business of games and fun, so we appreciate a good work-life balance, as well as company gatherings and celebrations, games, and unexpected foosball battles!

Murrieta is just a stone's throw from San Diego, Orange County and Los Angeles! The Murrieta/Temecula area is best known for its colorful hot air balloons, award winning wineries and championship golf courses settled in beautiful rolling hills. The city is just over 30 square miles on the north side of San Diego County offering a historic Downtown district, year-round entertainment and recreation, and an average temperature in the mid 70s.
